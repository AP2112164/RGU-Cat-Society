Charity

We work with Mrs Murray’s Cat & Dog Home!

Rehome a new best friend!

Meet friendly cats from Mrs Murray’s who are in need of a new home! You can meet them in the society on Wednesdays 1-4pm. There will be forms describing the personality of the cats and what environment they’d be happiest to live in. Forms can be filled out if you’d like to rehome a cat and you’ll recieve an email if your application is accepted!

Half profits go to charity!

50% of profits made from merchandise sales and events held by the Cat Society will go towards Mrs Murray’s Cat & Dog Home as we believe every pet deserves a happy home. The other 50% will be used to fund events, buy food/toys for the cats and to create merchandise. We hope you’ll buy from us to keep us and the cats very happy!

Charity links and info!
